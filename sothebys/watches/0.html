Property of a Scottish Noblewoman

A Victorian green painted and verdigris copper wall lantern

19th century


with original burner, curved glass body and glass base, glass with historic crack

74cm. high, 41cm. wide to fixing points, 28cm. deep; 2ft. 5in., 1ft. 4in., 11in.

A very decorative lantern and of pleasing size. Wear from being outside. Historic crack to curved glass body. Later painted. Ingrained dirt. Wear to surface showing copper.
